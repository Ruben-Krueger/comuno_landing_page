The only place to practice some languages. The best place to practice any language. Comuno at its heart is a language practicing service. Many languages have online courses and mobile apps dedicated to teaching them, but most languages don't have dedicated courses, online translating services or even decent practice material. Teaching all the world's languages is an impossible endeavor for a single person or group, but Comuno is the best place to practice all the world's languages, and the only place to find creatives and artists creating quality work intentionally by and for their linguistic community.
